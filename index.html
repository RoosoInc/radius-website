How Radius Works

Three steps. Zero effort. Complete visibility.

01

Place Radius on the Shelf

Set up your Radius unit where you stock cigarettes. Stack your packs on the tray as normal. Setup takes under a minute.

→
02

Packs Are Detected Automatically

Radius detects every pack added or removed in real time using built-in sensors — no scanning, no barcodes, no manual input.

→
03

Read the Count at a Glance

The bright LED display always shows your current stock. Know exactly when to reorder — without leaving the counter or opening a spreadsheet.
